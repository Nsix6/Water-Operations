# Business Goals

## Primary Business Goals

The primary business goals of the platform are:

- Reduce operational revenue leakage.
- Centralize customer ownership and customer interactions.
- Separate sales responsibilities from delivery responsibilities.
- Improve operational visibility across deliveries and payments.
- Digitize customer ordering workflows.
- Improve customer convenience and accessibility.
- Improve delivery accountability and traceability.
- Create a scalable infrastructure for future expansion.

---

# Operational Goals

The platform should:

- Allow customers place orders without relying on drivers.
- Ensure all payments go directly to the business.
- Restrict drivers to delivery-related responsibilities only.
- Provide centralized operational monitoring.
- Maintain traceable order and delivery histories.
- Improve reporting and operational analytics.

---

# Customer Experience Goals

The system should:

- Simplify the ordering process.
- Provide delivery visibility.
- Provide reliable order confirmations.
- Reduce delays and communication issues.
- Allow customers track order history.
- Improve trust and reliability.

---

# Long-Term Strategic Goals

Future strategic goals include:

- Multi-provider support
- Marketplace architecture
- Loyalty and rewards systems
- Mobile applications
- Route optimization
- Inventory management
- Real-time delivery tracking
- API integrations
- Operational analytics and forecasting

---

# Success Metrics

The success of the platform may be measured using:

- Reduction in operational discrepancies
- Increased direct customer orders
- Improved delivery completion rates
- Reduced delivery disputes
- Increased customer retention
- Faster delivery processing times
- Improved operational transparency
